[[Zertifikate|Server-Zertifikate]] increase security on the internet and are used to verify the authenticity of websites. A [[Zertifikate|Zertifikat]] is issued and verified by an official, trustworthy institution. A [[Zertifikate|Zertifikat]] cannot be forged. In everyday life, a [[Zertifikate|Zertifikat]] can be compared to an identity card or a notarized document. Similar to a passport, [[Zertifikate|Zertifikate]] are also valid for a certain period of time. Thus [[Zertifikate|Zertifikate]] make it possible to exchange data via secure connections and form an important basis of trust for operators of online shops.
