Deactivate the [[Zertifikate|Zertifikat]] while it is not yet valid. Your website will then not be certified, but at least visitors will not receive a warning message when they open your website. Alternatively, it is possible to use your old [[Zertifikate|Zertifikat]] while it is still valid. For information on how to renew [[Zertifikate|Zertifikate]], please refer to: [[Zertifikat-Abgelaufen/DE|Zertifikat abgelaufen]].
