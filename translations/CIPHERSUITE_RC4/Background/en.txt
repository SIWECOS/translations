The encryption algorithm RC4, which was very widely used for a long time, has been regarded as insecure for many years. Security experts know many critical flaws that have been used for cyber attacks. In 2015, the IETF (Internet Engineering Task Force) prohibited the use of RC4 for [[Transport Layer Security|TLS-Verbindungen]] in [https://tools.ietf.org/html/rfc7465 RFC7465].
