If the [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/RC4 RC4] encryption method is used, attackers can easily decode the communication between your website and your customer's browser, because RC4 has well-known security flaws. Information such as passwords, data entered in forms, or credit card information can be intercepted and misused for criminal purposes. This type of attack is called [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Man-in-the-middle_attack man-in-the-middle-attack].
