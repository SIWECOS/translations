[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Social_engineering_(security)#Phishing] is a method by which the visitor of a website is tricked into revealing sensitive personal data, such as passwords, credit card numbers, and personal information, to an attacker. Criminals usually start by sending [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Email_spam email spam]. These emails contain links to a phishing site that is designed to look like a legitimate website.
