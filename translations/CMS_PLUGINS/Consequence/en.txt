[[Content-Management-Systeme]] (CMS) offer a wide range of extensions. Vulnerabilities and security flaws in plugins are often exploited by attackers to gain control of a CMS installation or to misuse it for other purposes. Unfortunately, these security flaws are unavoidable in software. A large attack surface is a disadvantage that should be avoided.
